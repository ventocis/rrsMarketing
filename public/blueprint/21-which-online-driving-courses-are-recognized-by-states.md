# Which online driving courses are recognized by state agencies?

**Short answer:** Only **state‑approved** courses qualify for ticket benefits. In **Florida**, confirm **FLHSMV‑approved BDI** providers; in **Michigan**, take **BDIC** from an **approved sponsor** and **pass within 60 days** of SOS notification.

## What to do next
- **Florida:** Verify BDI rules and find the **approved list** on FLHSMV.
- **Michigan:** Read your **SOS notice** and use an **approved BDIC sponsor**.
- **Keep receipts** and follow any county/court instructions.

## Sources (official)
- FLHSMV — **Driver Improvement Schools (BDI)**: https://www.flhsmv.gov/driver-licenses-id-cards/education-courses/driver-improvement-schools/
- FLHSMV — **BDI overview & approved listing**: https://www.flhsmv.gov/driver-licenses-id-cards/education-courses/driver-improvement-schools/basic-driver-improvement-bdi-find-approved-listing-bdi-course-providers/
- Michigan SOS — **BDIC eligibility**: https://www.michigan.gov/sos/license-id/bdic
