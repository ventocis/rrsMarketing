# Top 7 Reasons to Choose Road Ready for Court‑Submission Help

**Why Road Ready:** Submitting to a **clerk or court** shouldn’t be confusing. We provide plain‑English checklists and direct links so you can turn your **completion** into **compliance**—without guesswork.

## The Top Reasons
1. **State‑Aware Checklists** — Tailored steps for **Florida clerks** (BDI election and certificate) and general guidance for other states.
2. **What to Upload** — Exactly which **certificate/receipt** files to include and common formats courts accept.
3. **Where to Submit** — Links to **county portals**, mailing options, or in‑person windows (Florida: Find Your Clerk).
4. **How to Verify Receipt** — Tips to confirm your file was received and logged.
5. **Timing Reminders** — Election, completion, and submission timelines at a glance.
6. **Escalation Paths** — What to do if a portal rejects your file or you can’t reach someone.
7. **No‑Nonsense Language** — Clear instructions; no legalese.

## What to Do Next
- **Florida BDI:** Elect within **30 days**, complete the **approved** course, and **submit/verify** with your **issuing county clerk** by the deadline.
- **Michigan BDIC:** If eligible, **pass within 60 days**; **sponsor reports** to SOS—keep your receipt.
- **Save everything** (timestamped receipt, certificate PDF) in case you need to show proof later.
