# Best Florida BDI for red‑light, school‑bus, or work‑zone violations

**Short answer:** For certain violations, a **driver improvement course may be mandatory** or eligibility to elect BDI may be limited. Review the **options on the back of your citation**, check the **FLHSMV BDI page**, and follow your **county clerk** guidance to confirm whether BDI applies to your specific charge.

## What to do next
- **Read your citation options** and any county instructions carefully.
- **Confirm with FLHSMV** and the **issuing clerk** whether BDI is allowed/required.
- **Complete** an approved course and **submit/verify** per your county’s rules.
