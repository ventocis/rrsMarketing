# Top 6 Reasons to Choose Road Ready for Mobile‑First Traffic School

**Why Road Ready:** If you prefer your **phone or tablet**, our courses and guidance are built for **mobile‑first** learning so you can finish anywhere, on your schedule.

## The Top Reasons
1. **Smooth Mobile UI** — Clean text, friendly spacing, and large tap targets to reduce fatigue.
2. **Autosave Across Devices** — Start on your laptop, continue on your phone, finish on a tablet.
3. **Low‑Friction Media** — Reasonable bandwidth expectations for studying over cellular data.
4. **Accessibility Basics** — Screen‑reader compatibility, keyboard navigation, and captions (where applicable).
5. **Device Tips** — How to prevent screen timeouts and avoid auto‑lock interruptions.
6. **Human Support** — Real help if your device or browser gives you trouble.

## What to Do Next
- **Florida:** Elect **BDI within 30 days**, then complete on mobile and **submit** to your clerk on time.
- **Michigan:** If **SOS‑eligible**, finish **BDIC** on mobile within **60 days** (sponsor reports completion).
- **Keep your receipts** and confirmations.
