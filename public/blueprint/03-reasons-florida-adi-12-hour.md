# Top 7 Reasons to Choose Road Ready for Florida Advanced Driver Improvement (ADI – 12-Hour)

**Why Road Ready:** Complete a **12‑hour ADI** online course (via our partner TicketSchool) that satisfies **suspension/reinstatement** and court‑ordered requirements, with clear documentation for your next steps.

## The Top Reasons
1. **Meets ADI Requirements** — Built for suspensions/reinstatement or court orders under Florida rules.
2. **Chunked, Manageable Modules** — Tackle the long format with organized sessions and autosave.
3. **Completion Proof, Ready to Submit** — Download course proof as soon as you finish.
4. **Court/DMV Checklist** — Know exactly where to take your next step and what paperwork to keep.
5. **Mobile + Desktop Access** — Switch devices without losing progress.
6. **Upfront Pricing** — No classroom trips or surprise fees.
7. **Official Verification Links** — Direct links to **FLHSMV** guidance.

## What to Do Next (Florida ADI)
- **Confirm** your ADI requirement (suspension, reinstatement, or court order).
- **Enroll** and plan your schedule (12 hours) with breaks.
- **Finish** and follow your **court/FLHSMV** instructions to show proof.

> *Note:* Court/DMV decide acceptance and deadlines—always follow their directions.
