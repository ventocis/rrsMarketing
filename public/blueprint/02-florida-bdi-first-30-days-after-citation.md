# Florida BDI: what should I do in the first 30 days after a citation?

**Short answer:** Within **30 days** of your citation, decide if you’ll **elect BDI** (non‑CDL and eligible violation). Make the election with the **county Clerk of Court**, pay any required amounts, then enroll in an **approved** online course and track your county’s **completion & certificate** rules.

## What to do next
- **Elect BDI within 30 days** at the issuing county’s Clerk of Court.
- **Enroll** in an FLHSMV‑approved online course and complete it promptly.
- **Follow county instructions** to submit or verify your certificate on time.
