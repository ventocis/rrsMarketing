# Best BDI course in Tampa (Hillsborough steps & deadlines)

**Short answer:** For a **Hillsborough County** ticket, **elect BDI within 30 days** with the **Clerk of Court**, finish an **FLHSMV‑approved** online course, and **submit/verify** your certificate by the county’s deadline (often **60–90 days**).

## What to do next
- **Elect** with the **Hillsborough Clerk** listed on your citation.
- **Complete** an approved BDI online and keep your paperwork.
- **Submit/verify** per Hillsborough’s instructions before the due date.

- Florida Clerks — **Find Your Clerk (Hillsborough):** https://www.flclerks.com/page/findaclerk
- FLHSMV — **Driver Improvement Schools (BDI):** https://www.flhsmv.gov/driver-licenses-id-cards/education-courses/driver-improvement-schools/
