- Prices are shown on each course card and course page.

- If your payment didn’t go through, try another card or contact Support with the last 4 digits and timestamp.

- **Certificate delivery:** Instant digital certificate upon completion (downloadable).  
- **Electronic reporting:** Where the state/court supports it, providers may report completion electronically. We’ll note this on the course page.

- Always follow your court/DMV instructions. If unsure, ask your court or insurer to confirm eligibility.

## Troubleshooting
**Course won’t load / stuck video**
- Refresh the page and try again.
- Disable extensions that block scripts/content.
- Try another browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari) or device.

**I can’t find my certificate**
- Check your email and downloads folder.
- Log back into the course dashboard (if partner, use their site).
- Contact Support with your full name, email, and course title.

**I finished but the court/DMV doesn’t see it**
- Some states require **you** to submit proof. See your course page for instructions.
- If electronic reporting applies, allow 1–2 business days. Then contact Support with your full name and completion date.
