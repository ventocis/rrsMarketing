# Top 7 Reasons to Choose Road Ready When You Need to Finish Today (Fast‑Track Options)

**Why Road Ready:** Need to move **fast**? We help you start immediately, complete as quickly as your course and state rules allow, and **ship you to the right next step** (clerk or SOS) so you meet the deadline.

## The Top Reasons
1. **Instant Enrollment** — Begin your online course right away—no waiting for classroom dates.
2. **Same‑Day Completion (where allowed)** — Short, focused modules so you can finish in a single sitting if permitted.
3. **Immediate Proof** — Download completion confirmation ASAP so you can submit without delay.
4. **Deadline Checklists** — Simple checklists for **Florida BDI election & clerk submission** and **Michigan BDIC 60‑day passes**.
5. **Device‑Friendly** — Start on one device, finish on another; autosave keeps your progress.
6. **Support When It Matters** — Humans to help if you’re under the gun.
7. **Official Links** — Straight to **FLHSMV/Clerks** and **Michigan SOS** so you submit correctly.

## What to Do Next
- **Florida ticket:** **Elect BDI** with your **county clerk within 30 days**, complete the approved course, then **submit/verify** by the county deadline.
- **Michigan ticket:** **Wait for SOS eligibility**, then **pass BDIC within 60 days** with an approved sponsor (reporting handled by the sponsor).
- **Keep receipts** and confirmations for your records.
