# Which online course works best on my phone (iOS/Android)?

**Short answer:** Many **approved** Florida BDI and Michigan BDIC providers offer **mobile‑friendly** platforms. Before enrolling, confirm **device compatibility**, **progress saving**, **practice tests**, and **support hours**—especially if you’re working against a **30‑day election** (FL) or **60‑day completion** (MI) deadline.

## What to do next
- **Florida:** Confirm BDI eligibility/election and choose an **approved** provider.
- **Michigan:** If **SOS‑eligible**, choose an **approved sponsor** and start early.
- **Test your device** and keep your login handy to study in small sessions.
