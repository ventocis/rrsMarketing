# Best BDI for snowbirds with out‑of‑state insurance (Florida ticket)

**Short answer:** If you received a **Florida** moving citation (and you’re **non‑CDL**), you can often **elect BDI within 30 days** with the **issuing county clerk**, complete an **approved** course online, and **submit** per county rules. Then ask your **home‑state insurer** how the outcome affects your policy.

## What to do next
- **Elect BDI** with the **Florida county clerk** listed on your ticket.
- **Complete an approved** BDI online and **submit/verify** on time.
- **Call your insurer** in your home state about any premium impact.
