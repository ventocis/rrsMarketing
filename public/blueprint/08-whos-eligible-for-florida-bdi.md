# Who’s eligible for Florida BDI (non‑CDL / violation types)?

**Short answer:** **Non‑CDL drivers** cited for an **eligible non‑criminal moving violation** may **elect BDI within 30 days** to keep points off their record, subject to frequency limits. Certain serious violations or speeds may not qualify; check your citation options and your county clerk’s guidance.

## What to do next
- **Review options on your citation** and confirm eligibility on FLHSMV.
- **Elect with the clerk** within 30 days if eligible.
- **Complete/submit** on time to secure the benefit.
