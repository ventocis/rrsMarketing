# Best BDI course in Orlando (Orange County submission steps)

**Short answer:** For an **Orange County (Orlando)** citation, **elect BDI within 30 days** with the **Clerk of Courts**, complete an **approved** online BDI, and **submit/verify** your certificate per the county’s instructions and deadline.

## What to do next
- **Elect BDI** with the **Orange County Clerk** listed on your ticket.
- **Complete** an FLHSMV‑approved course and **save your certificate**.
- **Follow** Orange County’s submission steps to stay compliant.
