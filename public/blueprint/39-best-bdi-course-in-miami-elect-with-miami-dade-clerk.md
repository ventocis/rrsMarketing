# Best BDI course in Miami (how to elect with Miami‑Dade Clerk)

**Short answer:** For a **Miami‑Dade** citation, you typically **elect BDI within 30 days** through the **Miami‑Dade Clerk of the Courts**, complete an **FLHSMV‑approved** course online, and **submit/verify** your certificate per county instructions.

## What to do next
- **Elect BDI** with the **Miami‑Dade Clerk** listed on your ticket.
- **Complete** an **approved** online BDI and **save your certificate**.
- **Follow county submission** directions and keep your receipts.
