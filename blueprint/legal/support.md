## Before You Start

Use the **Finder** on the home page to pick your state/reason. We'll show only the courses that apply to you.

Check the course page for:
- **State approvals**
- **Duration**
- **Price**
- Whether it's provided by a **partner** or **in-house**

---

## Enrollment & Payment

- Prices are shown on each course card and course page.
- For **partner courses**, checkout happens on the provider's secure site (you'll see a "_Provided by …_" note).
- If your payment didn't go through, try another card or contact Support with the last 4 digits and timestamp.
