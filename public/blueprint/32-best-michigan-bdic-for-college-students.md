# Best Michigan BDIC for college students on a deadline

**Short answer:** Once the **SOS notifies you**, you have **60 days** to **enroll, complete, and pass** BDIC with an **approved sponsor**. Choose a course with reliable **mobile/desktop** access so you can complete modules between classes—and **finish early** to avoid deadline stress.

## What to do next
- **Check your SOS notice** for the 60‑day window.
- **Choose an approved sponsor** with mobile access and progress saving.
- **Plan short sessions** and aim to finish in the first 2–3 weeks.
