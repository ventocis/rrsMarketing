## 1. Introduction and Scope

Road Ready Driver Instruction LLC, doing business as Road Ready Safety ("Road Ready Safety," "we," "us," or "our") operates www.roadreadysafety.com and related subdomains (collectively, the "Site"), as well as associated online driver safety education services, mobile applications, and course delivery platforms (together, the "Services").

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect information about individuals who access or use the Services, including:

- Registered students and course purchasers
- Website visitors and prospective users
- Job applicants and business contacts
- Representatives of partner organizations

Road Ready Safety is currently licensed or actively seeking licensure as a driver safety education provider in California, Florida, and Texas, and operates as a referral partner with Ticket School (ticketschool.com) and Driver Training Associates (drivertrainingassociates.com) for course delivery in other states. Your use of third-party partner platforms is subject to their separate privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies before enrolling through a referred provider.

This Privacy Policy applies to users in the United States only. By accessing the Services, you agree to the practices described below. If you do not agree, do not use the Services.

## 6. Data Security and Infrastructure

Road Ready Safety hosts its production platform on Amazon Web Services (AWS) and employs the following security practices:

- Encryption in transit: all data transmitted between users and our platform uses TLS 1.2 or higher
- Encryption at rest: sensitive data stored in our cloud databases and storage is encrypted using AES-256
- Access controls: role-based access controls (RBAC), least-privilege policies, and multi-factor authentication (MFA) for administrative access
- Logging and monitoring: cloud-based logging and monitoring services are used to maintain audit trails and detect anomalies
- Vulnerability management: regular security reviews and patching

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. You transmit data to us at your own risk. We recommend using a secure, private connection when accessing your account or submitting sensitive information.

## 9. State-Specific Regulatory Disclosures

As a licensed or applicant driver safety education provider, we are subject to specific regulatory requirements in the following states:

### 9.1 California

Road Ready Safety is seeking approval as a Traffic Violator School (TVS) operator and/or Basic Driver Improvement course provider under California Vehicle Code Section 11200 et seq. and DMV regulations. Once licensed, we will share required student data with the California DMV for purposes of license point dismissal processing and regulatory compliance. Such data sharing is mandatory and may not be subject to deletion requests under the CCPA.

### 9.2 Texas

Road Ready Safety is seeking licensure as a driver safety course provider under Texas Transportation Code Chapter 1001 and Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR) rules. Once licensed, we will report course completion data to TDLR and to courts as required. Student data may be shared with the Texas Department of Public Safety (DPS) in connection with citation dismissal processing.

### 9.3 Florida

Road Ready Safety is seeking approval as a Basic Driver Improvement (BDI) course provider under Florida Statutes Section 318.1951 and DHSMV regulations. Once licensed, we will report course completion data to the Florida DHSMV and to courts of jurisdiction as required. Such reporting may include your identity, citation information, and course completion status.

### 9.4 All Other States - Referral Partners

For users in states where Road Ready Safety is not yet a licensed provider, we may refer you to Ticket School (ticketschool.com) or Driver Training Associates (drivertrainingassociates.com). When you click through to a referral partner's site or complete a course with them, your information is governed by their privacy policies. We may receive limited data (such as referral tracking and course completion confirmation) from these partners to maintain our business relationship.
