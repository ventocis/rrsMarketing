# Best Michigan BDIC for mobile or tablet completion

**Short answer:** BDIC is delivered by **approved sponsors**. Many offer **mobile‑friendly** courses you can complete on a phone or tablet. Before you buy, confirm **device compatibility**, **progress saving**, and **support hours** so you can finish comfortably within the **60‑day** window.

## What to do next
- **Confirm eligibility** from SOS and note your **deadline**.
- **Pick an approved sponsor** with mobile support and progress tracking.
- **Start early** and plan a few shorter study sessions on your device.
