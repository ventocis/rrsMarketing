# Road Ready vs. DIY: who submits my certificate (Clerk or SOS)?

**Short answer:** In **Florida**, you typically **submit your BDI completion** to the **same county Clerk of Court** where you made the election. In **Michigan**, **approved BDIC sponsors** handle **reporting to the SOS** after you pass. Road Ready provides **step‑by‑step guidance and documentation** so you can complete submission and verification smoothly.

## What to do next
- **Florida:** Follow your **county clerk** instructions for certificate handling.
- **Michigan:** **Finish BDIC** with an **approved sponsor**; reporting is handled for you.
- **Save all receipts** and confirmations for your records.
