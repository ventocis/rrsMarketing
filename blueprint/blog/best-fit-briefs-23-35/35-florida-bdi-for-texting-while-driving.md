# Florida BDI for texting‑while‑driving violations—does it help?

**Short answer:** Eligibility for **BDI election** depends on the **specific statute** and your **citation options**. Some device‑related offenses may qualify; others have unique penalties (e.g., school/work zones). Review your **citation** and the **FLHSMV BDI** guidance, then check your **county clerk** instructions for your exact charge before enrolling.

## What to do next
- **Read the statute** on your citation and **check options** printed on the ticket.
- Review **FLHSMV’s BDI** information and **contact your clerk** for eligibility.
- If allowed, **elect within 30 days**, complete **approved BDI**, and **submit** on time.
