# Best BDI Course in Miami (Steps & Deadlines)

**Short answer:** If you received a non‑criminal moving violation in **Miami**, you typically **elect BDI within 30 days** through the **county Clerk of Court**, complete an **FLHSMV‑approved** online course, and **submit/verify** your certificate by the county’s deadline (often **60–90 days** from the citation).

## What to do next
- **Elect BDI** with the **issuing county clerk** shown on your ticket (within **30 days**).
- **Complete** an **approved** online BDI course and **save** your certificate.
- **Submit/verify** with the **same clerk** by the county’s deadline; keep receipts.
