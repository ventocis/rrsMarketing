**Short answer:** In **Ann Arbor/Washtenaw County**, **BDIC** eligibility is set by **Michigan SOS**. If you’re eligible, you have **60 days** to **enroll, complete, and pass** with an **approved sponsor**; passes are **reported to SOS**.
