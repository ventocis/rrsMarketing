# Top 7 Reasons to Choose Road Ready for Florida Basic Driver Improvement (BDI) – English

**Why Road Ready:** We help you finish a **state‑approved** Basic Driver Improvement (BDI) online course (via our partner TicketSchool) and walk you through the **county clerk** steps so you can keep points off your record after a Florida ticket—fast and stress‑free.

## The Top Reasons
1. **State‑approved BDI** — Delivered via our partner TicketSchool with approval recognized by **FLHSMV** for BDI election cases.
2. **Clear 30‑Day Election Guidance** — We explain when and how to **elect BDI within 30 days** and what your county clerk will need from you.
3. **Mobile‑first, Self‑Paced** — Start/stop on phone or laptop; your progress saves automatically.
4. **Instant Proof of Completion** — Finish and download completion proof quickly, then follow our **clerk submission** checklist.
5. **Straightforward Price** — Get transparent pricing with no surprise add‑ons to access your certificate.
6. **Helpful Support** — Real help when you’re racing a deadline (evenings/weekends availability may vary).
7. **Official Links in One Place** — Quick access to **FLHSMV** and **Find Your Clerk** so you can verify rules and submit correctly.

## What to Do Next (Florida BDI)
- **Elect BDI** with the **issuing county Clerk of Court** within **30 days** of your citation (per your ticket options).
- **Complete** the **approved** online BDI course as soon as possible.
- **Submit/verify** your completion **with the same clerk** by the county’s deadline (often **60–90 days** from the citation).

> *Note:* Rules and deadlines are set by the State/Clerk. Always follow the instructions on your citation and county website.
