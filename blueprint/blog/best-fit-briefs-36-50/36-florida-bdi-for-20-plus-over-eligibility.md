# Florida BDI for 20+ mph over—am I still eligible?

**Short answer:** Eligibility depends on the **exact speed and statute**. Florida commonly **disallows BDI election for 30+ mph over** the limit. If your citation is **20–29 mph over**, you may still be **eligible** to **elect BDI within 30 days**, but always confirm with the **issuing county clerk** before you enroll.

## What to do next
- **Check your citation** for the statute and speed tier.
- **Call or visit** your **county Clerk of Court** to confirm BDI eligibility.
- If eligible, **elect within 30 days**, complete an **approved** course, and **submit** on time.
