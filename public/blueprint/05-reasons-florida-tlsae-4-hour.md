# Top 7 Reasons to Choose Road Ready for Florida Drug and Alcohol Course (TLSAE – 4-Hour)

**Why Road Ready:** Complete the **Traffic Law & Substance Abuse Education (TLSAE)** requirement online (via our partner TicketSchool) before getting your first Florida driver license.

## The Top Reasons
1. **Meets the Florida New‑Driver Requirement** — TLSAE is required before your license; complete it fully online.
2. **Beginner‑Friendly Lessons** — Built for first‑time drivers with clear, conversational modules.
3. **Mobile‑Ready & Self‑Paced** — Study on your phone or laptop with autosave.
4. **Instant Completion Proof** — Get documentation you can reference for your next DMV step.
5. **Pair with Knowledge Exam** — Tips for combining TLSAE with the **online Class E exam** (if you plan to test from home).
6. **Fair Pricing** — Upfront, honest cost.
7. **Official Links** — Go straight to **FLHSMV** resources for first‑time drivers.

## What to Do Next (TLSAE)
- **Enroll** in TLSAE and complete the 4‑hour course.
- **Decide** whether to take the **Class E knowledge exam online** or at a service center.
- **Follow FLHSMV** steps to finalize your learner’s/license application.

> *Note:* Testing rules and ID/proctoring vary—always follow **FLHSMV** instructions.
